Rain

Rain falls gently or fiercely, nourishing the earth. Each drop connects sky to ground, completing a cycle that sustains life. The sound of rain creates a rhythm that calms the mind.

Observation

Watch how rain transforms surfaces. Puddles form, leaves glisten, and the air becomes fresh. Rain washes away dust and brings renewal to the landscape.

Ecological Insight

Rain is essential for ecosystems. It replenishes water sources, supports plant growth, and maintains humidity. Water cycles demonstrate nature's efficient recycling systems.
