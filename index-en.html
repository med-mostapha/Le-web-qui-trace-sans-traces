Eco-Design & Experimentation

This minimal website embodies eco-design principles: simplicity, privacy, and a calm digital experience. Inspired by the Gemini Protocol, it demonstrates that meaningful content can exist without tracking, external dependencies, or unnecessary complexity.

Project Principles

  • One request per page — no external resources
  • Optional media loading — content never auto-loads
  • Text-first approach — clear, fast, accessible
  • Lightweight pages — under 50KB total
  • Terminal-compatible — works in text browsers
  • Minimal dependencies — hand-crafted HTML/CSS
